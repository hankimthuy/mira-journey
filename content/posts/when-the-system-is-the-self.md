Hi there, I'm Thuy.

If you've found me through my technical work, you probably see someone deeply immersed in code, workflows, and complex systems. My professional identity is a "Builder" — someone who loves making things run more efficiently.

However, after a few years in the industry, I noticed a strange paradox: we spend months optimizing performance for machines, yet we often let our own lives run on "default settings".

I started this space because I realized that humans are the most complex and fascinating "systems" of all.

## Not documentation — an inner blueprint

This isn't a space for technical documentation. It's a space where I explore how we organize our inner world and manage our own capacity. I'm deeply drawn to Adlerian psychology and Systems Thinking because they provide the blueprints for self-leadership.

I believe we all have an "Innerverse" that deserves as much attention as the software we build, especially in a world that pushes us toward unrealistic productivity.

## What I'll be writing here

- **Life Reflection**: Applying an organized mindset to look back and navigate personal growth with more clarity.
- **Psychology & Self-Leadership**: Navigating our daily energy and learning to lead ourselves without falling into the trap of burnout.
- **Touch-point — The Human Side of Tech**: The transition from building an application to building meaningful human experiences.

Thank you for joining me on this journey to find the harmony between logic and empathy.
